Cultivate

  • Cultivate (verb): Trồng trọt - To prepare land and grow crops on it, or to grow a particular crop.
  • Cultivate (verb): Trau dồi - To try to develop and improve something.
  • Cultivation (noun): Quá trình trồng trọt - Process of cultivating.
  • Cultivator (noun): Người/Công cụ trồng trọt - Person or tool that cultivates
  • Cultivating (gerund)
  • Cultivated (past simple)
  • Cultivated (past participle)

Tenses

Tenses in English is a form of Verb.
Tenses is used to describe time.
Tenses divided by time: Past tense, Present tense, Future Tense.
Tenses divided by state: Simple tense, Perfect tense, Continuous tense, Perfect continuous tense.

Present simple tense

  • Base form verb if subject is first/second person pronouns: I swim in river.
  • Add 's to verb if subject is third person pronouns: He/She/It swims in river

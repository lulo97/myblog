GRAMMAR MISTAKE

  • Vietnamese: Khi tôi đi bộ trên vỉa hè gần nhà của tôi. Tôi thấy một con mèo đang ngủ trên mái nhà hàng xóm của tôi.
  • Original: When I was walking in the street near my home this morning. I saw a cat sleeping in the roof of my neightboor.
  • Word mistake: neightbor --> neightbor
  • Preposition mistake: "in the street" --> "walking down the street"
  • Bonus preposition:
    • in the street = standing in middle of street waiting for car to hit you.
    • on the street = what put above of the road of street, like building on the street.
    • walking down the street = walking on the sidewalk
  • Preposition mistake: in the roof --> on the roof
  • Bonus: "in the roof of my neightboor" the same as "on the neighbor's roof"
